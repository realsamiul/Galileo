E. Advice on PageSpeed & GSAP Integration (Summary):
 * PageSpeed (Score 79, Render-Blocking):
   * Main culprits are often large CSS files loaded synchronously and sometimes fonts or unoptimized images/JS.
   * Webflow manages its core CSS/JS. Your control is mainly over:
     * Image Optimization: Crucial. Use WebP/AVIF, responsive images (srcset), lazy loading for below-the-fold, proper sizing.
     * Font Optimization: font-display: swap; (which you use) is key. Consider preloading critical fonts.
     * Minimize Custom Code in <head>: Defer/async custom JS.
     * Rely on Webflow's built-in optimizations (CDN, minification).
 * GSAP Integration & Template Choice (Relume vs. Others):
   * Relume: Good for structure and speed, but components can be "boring" initially and require significant styling.
   * Highly Styled Templates (like "LeStudio"): Visually strong but can be hard to "whittle down" and may have conflicting JS/interactions if adding heavy GSAP.
   * Bare Webflow Cloneables: Cleanest slate for GSAP, offering maximum control but requiring all styling from scratch.
   * Recommended Hybrid for GSAP: Start with a minimalist Webflow foundation, use Relume for initial structure only, apply custom styling extensively, then implement GSAP. This gives structural speed, design freedom, and a cleaner environment for GSAP compared to heavily pre-styled/interacted templates.
